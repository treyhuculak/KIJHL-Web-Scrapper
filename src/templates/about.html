About ZebraZone

ZebraZone is a specialized statistics and game-management tool designed specifically for hockey officials. Developed to streamline the way referees track their game history, career numbers, and events throughout the league, ZebraZone provides a centralized "hub" for the modern official.

Currently, the platform integrates data from the WHL and KIJHL, allowing officials to quickly look up the important game details without the hassle of scanning large boxscores. Whether you are reviewing your last game or scanning for exciting games throughout the league, ZebraZone is built to keep you in the zone.

Mission

Our mission is to empower the officiating community with data-driven insights, helping referees at all levels have instant access to the metrics that matter, as well as their own officiating history and league-wide statistics.
